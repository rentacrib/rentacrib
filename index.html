Section 01

What We Collect

When you use RentaCrib ZW, we may collect the following information:

  • Account data: Username and PIN (hashed) created during registration.
  • Listing data: Property details, photos, location, and contact number you submit when posting a listing.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, listings viewed, and search filters used — stored locally in your browser.

Information collected is used solely to operate and improve the Platform:
